Ready to join the team?

Only 15 spots. A real tryout. A whole season of building & competing. If you're up for the challenge, here's how to start.

★ Mission Possible · '25–26
★ Read this first ★

Only 15 spots.
And we want them earned.

Joining the team is a real commitment. Every student is tested before being accepted. Selection is competitive, and we're looking for students who'll show up, study, and chase a spot at the State competition.

The Process

Joining is as easy as 1, 2, 3.

1

Send us an email

Reach out to express interest, ask questions, or request more information. We respond to every message. No question is too small!

fallonscienceolympiad@gmail.com

2

Come to the info meeting

Learn about the program, meet the coaches, and ask all your questions. Parents are encouraged to attend with their student.

📅 Info Meeting: Mandatory to Join
Date: Friday, June 6 Time: 10:00–11:00 AM Where: Online via Zoom

⚠️ Attendance is required for anyone who wants to join the team next year. A Zoom link will be sent after you email us, so reach out soon!

3

Take the tryouts

Only 15 students make the team. Tryouts are real, graded, and competitive. They help us build balanced groups and place each student in events that fit their strengths and interests. Bring your A-game.

4

Commit for the year

Once accepted, students commit to the full season: practices, study sessions, builds, and competitions. Our goal: qualify for the State competition, and getting there takes everyone showing up. Parents commit to funding and transportation throughout the year.

☀️
Want a head start? Practice over summer. Students are encouraged to explore and practice events they're interested in before the season starts. It's a great way to build confidence and figure out what you enjoy. That said, summer practice does not guarantee a spot on the team. Everyone still goes through tryouts, and selection is based on performance there.
The Deal

What we expect.

A little something from both sides.

🎒

From students

  • Show up to practices and study sessions, every week
  • Own your events. Study them. Care about them.
  • Be a real partner to the people you're paired with
  • Bring effort and curiosity (we'll handle the rest)
  • Wear the Fallon shirt with pride
👪

From parents

  • Help fund the program (it's parent-funded)
  • Drive students to practices and competitions
  • Cheer your kid on through a long season
  • Reach out to us anytime. We love hearing from parents.
Where We Compete

Three levels of battle.

🎟️

Invitationals

Other schools' practice tournaments. Lower-stakes, big learning, and honestly some of the most fun comp days.

🌎

Regionals

The first official qualifier. Top teams move on to State. That's the goal.

🏆

State & Nationals

The big stage. State teams from every region; nationals teams from every state. We're chasing it.
